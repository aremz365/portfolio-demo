About Me

I am an aerospace engineering and design graduate at the National Aerospace university Kharkiv, ukraine. I transitioned to mobile and web development. I made web development a starting point in my career back in 2019.

I started my design career in visual and user Interface design then added UX design to my portfolio. My goal is to work on as many projects as possible that is user centred and solve real world complex problems

Beyond professional work, I also love connecting with other creatives. I find myself inspired and motivated by the works of my fellows. I'm always eager to grow and learn as a designer.

In my free time, I enjoy watching documentaries, playing video games and also playing soccer.

SpaceUX case study was based on the Dunning-Kruger Effect.


SpaceUX is a personal project I was really keen on developing, it allowed to narrate a story that represents a shift in the design industry post pandemic. I was able to have meaningful conversations with some of the participants on a wide range of topics about their current career decisions, goals and passion most importantly their limitations/ fear. I was able to gain invaluable experience (people skill) during the course of writing the research.
